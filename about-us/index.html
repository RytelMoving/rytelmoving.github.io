About Us

Rytel Moving and Storage is a licensed and insured in the state of Minnesota (HHG# 171415).

We have multiple trucks, experienced movers and all of the supplies for a safe and timely move.

Rytel Moving and Storage is owned and managed by Stan and Grace Rytel out of Willmar, Minnesota.

Starting in 1987, Stan worked with two national moving companies: Mayflower Moving and United Van Lines. He started Polamer Moving Services in 1995. In 2013, Polamer Moving changed it’s name to Rytel Moving and Storage due to expansion of storage services.
